About


Welcome to Beckman High School Link Cru. Link Cru is a high school organization dedicated to acclimating our new students into the school
environment. Whether you are a freshman or transfer student, Link Leaders will ensure an easy transition through school opportunities to get
new students involved on campus. Leaders will encourage new students to find their passions and feel connected to the diverse community at
Beckman High School. Go Patriots!


Mr. Goldenberg, BHS Link Cru Director
